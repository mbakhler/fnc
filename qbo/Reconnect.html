Reconnect

It seems that you’ve disconnected ChefMod from your QuickBooks company. Unfortunately, ChefMod can no longer feed your accounting process with vital information, and you will have to enter it all manually in QuickBooks. Perhaps you have done it in error. Don’t worry, it happens to the best of us. You can reconnect at any time following these simple steps below.

  1. Login to ChefMod
  2. Click on “Financials” tab.
  3. Click on Setup button.
  4. On the left, under “Connect Systems” section, click on “QuickBooks Online” link.
  5. Select a location you want to reconnect, click on button and follow QuickBooks instructions.
